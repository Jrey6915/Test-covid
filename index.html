What is Covid-19?

What is Covid-19? The Corona virus or mostly known as Covid-19 is a new type of "SARS-COV-2" that affects your respiratory system through Human interactions. The origins of this disease is still not determined do to the fact that scientists are still speculating if the virus really originated on "Wuhan"China and also we still don't know if bats really passed the virus to us. In this video it informs a lot more that we know about the virus. As of lately scientists around the world have developed types of vaccines to further prevent the spread of the virus.


What are the symptom's

Symptoms:
Common symptoms:
  • Fever
  • Dry coughs
  • Tiredness
Less common symptoms
  • Aches and Pains
  • Sore Throat
  • diarrhea
  • Conjunctivitis
  • Headaches
  • The loss of Taste or Smell
  • Rashes
Severe symptoms:
  • The difficulty to breath
  • Chest Pain
  • The loss of speech
  • Mobility
If the person shows any of this severe symptoms contact your local doctor immediately.

Why is it important to spread awareness

The reasons of why we should spread awareness? It is important for us as a community to spread awareness because Covid-19 can cause serious and deadly illness. We must protect our selfs and various others from getting sick, if you or a loved one starts developing symptoms it is best to reduce the risk of involving a lot of people from getting infected.
