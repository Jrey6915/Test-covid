Ways to prevent Covid-19

Home Prevention Diagram Form


Protect yourself and others around you by knowing the facts and taking appropriate precautions. Follow advice provided by your local health authority.
Here are some of the practices provided by your local health authority:
  • Washing your hands
  • Being 6 feet apart
  • Cleaning and disinfecting
  • Wear a mask
  • Prevent from touching your face
  • Stay away from large groups of people
If you are infected, these are some of the protocols to follow:
  • Quarantine yourself
  • Contact your Doctor before visiting
  • Avoid any human interactions
  • Rest and Hydration
  • Drink a lot of Vitamin-C
